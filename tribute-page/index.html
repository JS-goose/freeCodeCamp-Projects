Jeremy Sexton

A man who taught me a lot about life

Here is a timeline of Jeremy's life:

  • 1984 - Born in Virginia to the tune of 8lbs 4oz
  • 1986 - I was born, I'm sure this changed his life having an annoying little brother :D
  • 1989 - Starts school, 2nd younger brother is born
  • 1991 to 2001 - Fairly uneventful life up until high school where he continually became an uneasy child to raise and got into trouble
  • 2002 - Started getting into major trouble in school, after an incident was expelled and put into state's custody
  • 2003 - Gets out of state's custody and meets the mother of his children
  • 2004 - First child is born and she is the light of his life
  • 2006 - Second child is born, even more light!
  • 2008 - After several bouts of depression and drug abuse, is admitted to psychiatric hospital
  • 2009 - Moves to Texas with me to start a new life after some legal and marital troubles
  • 2010 - New job, new car, having fun and loving life!
  • 2012 - Depression begins to rear its ugly head again making life difficult
  • Late 2012 - Starts depending on alcohol as depression worsens
  • Early 2013 - Things start to look up
  • Mid 2013 Depression and alcohol make for a volitile mix, doesn't want help after many attempts
  • October 2013 - Ends life by suicide :( Leaves behind two beautiful daughters, three brothers, a mother, and a world that is much darker now that he is gone

If you or anyone you know is suffering from depression, please -- please get help. Get help from family, from wives, from anyone willing to listen. If you feel like there is no way out, I implore you to reconsider; don't make a permanent decision to temporary problems.
